Elisa Steak House
62 Maple Avenue, Barrie, ON L4N 1R8
Chinese

My daughter and I decided it was time to get some good steaks. Unfortunately, Vancouver is bereft of truly excellent steaks since Chef Mohammed left Tibisti a few years ago. The price is ridiculous, it's not somewhere we will eat regularly (or ever?) but... the steaks were truly outstanding. As is mentioned in the Halal Summary above, certain suppliers provide Halal meats. I checked each supplier's website to confirm. What really impressed me though, was that the waiter was extremely knowledgeable and helpful despite being non-Muslim. When we ordered only from those suppliers, he guessed we wanted Halal (which we confirmed) and then told us he would request the 'red wine demi-glaze'...

Ali Ahmad

Sammamish, WA
